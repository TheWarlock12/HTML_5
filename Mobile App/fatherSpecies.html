Father Species

In the benining there was 8, the Demcaeli flew in the skys above The Dwarves dug great halls in the earth The Eeffreet in their canons of boiling molton stone The Merfolk in their oceans below The Vampires in forests as gloomy as night the centaur in great plains rode out of sight the Giants as old as the stone, shake the ground as they stamp their great feet And Finally the Humans all they will meet, and become friends with the fathers till worlds end
